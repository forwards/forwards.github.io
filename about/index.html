R Foundation taskforce on women and other under-represented groups

Background

The task force was set up by the R Foundation in December 2015 to address the underrepresentation of women among contributors to the R project and in the R community more generally.

The members of the task force were invited to participate based on their interest in addressing the gender imbalance and their contributions in this area, as well as to give a reasonable balance of different genders, different geographic regions, different work contexts, different career stages and different aspects of the R project (e.g.Â CRAN and Bioconductor).

The task force is happy to engage with other individuals and organisations (such as R-ladies) that are concerned about this issue and actively seeks outside contributions on specific tasks.
