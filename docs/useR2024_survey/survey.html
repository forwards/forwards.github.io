Author

Dillon Sparks

Disability

(Q: Do you have any disabilities or impairments that affect the way you work/study or place special requirements on your working/learning environment?)

6% of respondents (n = 14) declared a disability or impairment that affects the way they work or study.

(Q: What kind of disability do you have?)

The following disabilities were represented among useR! 2024 participants:

  • A vision impairment
  • A hearing impairment
  • A mobility impairment
  • A learning disability
  • A mental health disorder
  • Other disability

Participants specified the following under “Other disability”: dysautonomia, autism & ADHD .

LGBTQIA+ identity

(Q: Do you identify as LGBTQIA+?)

9% of respondents (n = 23) identified as lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, intersex, or another sexual or gender minority.
